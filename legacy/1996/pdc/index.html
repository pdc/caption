Damian's photos

CAPTION96 took place in the summer of 1996, in our usual venue, the Oxford Union Society. Damian Cugley borrowed a primitive digital camera for the occasion. We have organized our selection from his pictures by the people in them (not counting some people who're obscured or not facing the camera).
