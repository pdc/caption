Workshops

Caption, Oxford's annual small-press comics convention, was held in the Oxford Union Society, Oxford, England on the 18th and 19th of August 2001.

Woodrow Phoenix's League of Lonely Hearts

Woodrow Phoenix will be running an interactive panel at Caption 2001 with superheros and romance as its inevitable theme. Audience participation will be required! Apparently this workshop format works well with 8-year-olds, so it should suit the CAPTION audience down to the ground...

Woodrow (with Ian Carney) does Sugar Buzz, published by Slave Labor.

Jeremy Dennis's Romance Comics Race

Jeremy's crazy plan is to thrash out a plot for a romance comic and then race three teams against each other to produce a finished story -- pen and paper versus a Polaroid camera versus a digital camera and borrowed laptop. With the romantic city of Oxford as a backdrop, how can we lose?

Jeremy Dennis is an amateur cartoonist living in Oxford -- as well as being one of the founding comittee members for CAPTION itself...
