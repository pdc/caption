Damian's photos from Caption 2001

Caption, Oxford's annual small-press comics convention, was held in the Oxford Union Society, Oxford, England on the 18th and 19th of August 2001.

These are Damian's photos -- or at least a few chosen from the mass of snaps taken on a borrowed Mavica (digital) camera.
