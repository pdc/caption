Saturday 11 and Sunday 12 August 2007

East Oxford Community Centre, Oxford

Call for Artwork for Caption 2007

Still time to get your pens & pencils out and let those imaginations loose for the CAPTION 2007 exhibition/auction. Theme: ‘Dreams or Nightmares’.

If you can't think of an idea on the theme then we’ll be putting some creative prompts on the website soon, such as common dream themes like flying, public nudity, buckets of mice, spiders, the fires of hell etc.

To be included in the CAPTION booklet please send 300-dpi scans to jay.eales@googlemail.com, or send good copies or original art to Jay Eales, 38 Clarkes Road, Wigston, Leics, LE18 2BE by Mid-July. If you can’t make that deadline, but would still like to provide a piece for the exhibition, please send the artwork to the above address by the 2nd August, or present it to the CAPTION registration desk when you arrive.

Please indicate when providing your artwork whether you would like it returned, or whether you would be prepared to donate it to the CAPTION Auction.

CAPTION Publicity: Selina Lock (selina.lock@googlemail.com) & Jay Eales (jay.eales@googlemail.com)

For regular updates on the event as CAPTION hoves into view, check out http://caption.org/2007/
