Caption 2007

Dreams & Nightmares

Saturday 11 and Sunday 12 August 2007

East Oxford Community Centre, Oxford

Panels and Presentations

A quick round-up of the panels and presentations form the CAPTION programme.

Representatives of the Montgolfiers (Richard Reynolds, Guy Lawley, and Jenni Scott) will be talking about Dreams and Nightmares in Comics—with slides! Saturday 15.30.

Woodrow Phoenix will be illustrating contestants tales of nightmares in the business in a variation on Play Your Cards Right called Living the Nightmare. Saturday 17.00.

Karen Rubins will be running a 24-Minute Comic game—for which she will need volunteers willing to draw pages. Sunday 14.00.

Oli Smith hosts a panel discussion of the intriguing thesis Fulfilling Your Dreams is a F*cking Nightmare. Sunday 15.00.


And don’t forget Tony Hitchman’s quiz on Saturday evening, and Al Davison’s Dream Workshop on both Saturday and Sunday.
