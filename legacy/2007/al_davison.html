Caption 2007

Dreams & Nightmares

Saturday 11 and Sunday 12 August 2007

East Oxford Community Centre, Oxford

Dream Workshop by Al Davison

Who better to run a workshop on dreams and comics than Al Davison, who has lead workshops at several past CAPTION conventions and is a member of the Somnambulists, an art movement that takes inspiration from dream imagery. Al has made comics, plays and films based on dreams and his own life, including the graphic novel Spiral Cage, about his growing up with spina-bifida.
