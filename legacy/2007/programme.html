Caption 2007

Dreams & Nightmares

Saturday 11 and Sunday 12 August 2007

East Oxford Community Centre, Oxford

Programme of Events

All weekend (10.00—01.00 on Saturday 11th, 11.00—18.00 on Sunday 12th August 2007)

Saturday

10.00 Doors open
10.00–12.00 Mooching, buying comics, looking round & drawing on the art pads.
12.00 Bar opens!
12.00–13.00 Lunch is readily available in Oxford’s finest street of many restaurants along the Cowley Road.
13.00–15.00 Al Davison Dream Comic Workshop
14.00–15.00 Living the Dream (with Daniel Merlin Goodbrey and Douglas Noble and others)
15.00–16.00 Montgolfiers—Guy Lawley, Jennis scott, and Richard Reynolds on Dreams & Nightmares in Comics
17.00–18.00 ‘Naked Artist’ Nightmares (with live illustrations by Woodrow Phoenix) and a chance to win one of five copies of Bryan Talbot’s new book, The Naked Artist)
18.30–20.00 Auction
20.00 Dinner Break
21.30–22.30 What’s my Monstervation? Quiz with Tony Hitchman
01.00 Doors close

Sunday

11.00 Doors open
14.00–15.00 24-Minute Comic Dash, hosted by Karen Rubins
15.00–16.00 Fulfilling your Dreams is a F*cking Nightmare—Oli Smith discusses this theme with a panel of small pressers.
15.00–17.00 Repeat—Al Davison Dream Comic Workshop
16.00–17.00 CAPTION—The Shape of Things To Come?
18.00 Doors close
