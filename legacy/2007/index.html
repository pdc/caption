CAPTION is a festival of comics and minicomics held annually in Oxford. Join us for a weekend of panels, workshops, and idle chatter in the bar. Bring your comics and leave them on the CAPTION table to be sold on your behalf, so you can enjoy the rest of the convention. Buy new treasures from the CAPTION table and the other incredibly select stalls.
