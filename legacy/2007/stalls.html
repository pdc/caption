Buying minicomics and zines at Caption

The CAPTION committe run their own table where you can deposit your comics to be sold for you while you enjoy the delights that CAPTION has to offer.
At the end of the day you collect any unsold comics and the takings, minus a 10% cut that goes towards CAPTION’s running costs.

Before BugPowder the web site, there was BugPowder the roving comix stall and mail-order distributor, run by Pete Ashton in the 1990s. The stock left over from the distro days that has not been claimed by its owners has now been donated to CAPTION to dsistrubute amongst the faithful ast low or no cost.
