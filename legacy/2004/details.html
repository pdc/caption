Details

CAPTION is a comics festival/convention held every Summer in Oxford. You can join in advance by printing out the application form, filling it in, and posting it to us. Or you can join on the door.

Standard membersip (Sat + Sun) is £10 or £5 (unwaged). Sunday-only memberships are available at £5. We also have a £20 Friends of CAPTION membership for generous supporters

Visit the CAPTION Community on LiveJournal to read more about CAPTION and alternative/small-press comics. If you have questions, send mail to caption2004@caption.org or post a letter to CAPTION 2004, 18 Hawkins Street, Oxford OX4 1YD.

Join the CAPTION mailing list to keep up to date.

The CAPTION web site has archived information about past CAPTION conventions reaching as far back as its murky beginnings back in the twentieth century.
