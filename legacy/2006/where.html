East Oxford Community Centre

Where to find us

Show on Google Maps or Multimap

The East Oxford Community Centre is 10–15 minutes’ walk from central Oxford: straight up the High Street and along Cowley Road.

Five minutes by bus: cat the 1, 5, 5A, or 5B from Quesn Street or Carfax. Ring the bell when you see a place called ‘Chicken Cottage’ on your left-hand side; there is a bus stop right outside the EOCC.
