CAPTION2006

A DIY comics festival in Oxford

5–6 August 2006

Wolfson College, Oxford

We like making comics

Print the application form

Join us at CAPTION for a weekend of comix-related workshops, panels, exhibitions, and other amusements. Relax in the bar, in the big garden, or even by the side of the River Cherwell. Buy comics from our very select collection of stallholders. Sell your your own comics via the CAPTION table: leave them with us and we will sell them for you (for a 10% fee), leaving you free to join in the fun.

This year CAPTION has a new committee and is looking for your ideas to add to the mix. The CAPTION-discuss mailing list is for everyone who wants to to discuss any aspect of the CAPTION convention. If email makes you shy, we plan to have people at as many comics events as we can for you to buttonhole and talk to about workshops, panels, or discussion topics

Where, when, how much

Show me on Google Maps

Wolfson College is a large graduate college of the University of Oxford situated in North Oxford beside the River Cherwell.

For CAPTION we have a large room with a screen for slide shows, tables for stall-holders to sell comix, and a room for panels and workshops—not to mention the student-run bar, which conveniently is decorated in the style of Roy Lichtenstein, and gardens with an artificial lake and island for punts.

The con opens on the morning of Saturday 5 August 2006 and closes the following Sunday afternoon.

Admission is £10 for the whole weekend, or £5 for one day.
