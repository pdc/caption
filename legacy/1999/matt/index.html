CAPTION >> 1999 >> matt

spacecaption1999 took place on Saturday and Sunday 21-22 August 1999, in our usual venue, the Oxford Union Society. Matt Brooker took his collection of cameras to CAPTION. We have organized our selection from his pictures by the people in them (not counting some people who’re obscured or not facing the camera). There also is a list of the web pictures in alphabetical order.
