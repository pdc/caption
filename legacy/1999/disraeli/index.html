CAPTION >> 1999 >> disraeli

We invited comics artists to donate strips of up to three pages for the spacecaption1999 programme booklet. D’Israeli seized on this as a chance to experiment with the production of a strip entirely within the informational womb of the computer. After CAPTION D’Isaeli’s fired up his babbage engine again to produce the tinted version of his strip you see here.
