CAPTION2000

Saturday-Sunday 12-13 August 2000, at the Oxford Union Society, Oxford, England


Still life can hear tiny objects of fear

The exhibition

Caption's exhibition is about trawling deep into the small things which scatter through your life, startling and scaring and setting the mind on edge. Contributions should be small (13cmsq or less), deeply disturbing, and not so heavy that they can't be hung from the ceiling with surgical cotton and bent pins. If what you're sending in is sticky, please be polite enough to shrink-wrap it (many copy-shops will now shrink-wrap things for you) to avoid soiling. That said, I will try my best to display everything you send in, so if your tiny object of fear starts to swell, it's no cause for anxiety.

If you've prepared something else, or don't like the theme, Caption desperately needs items for its charity auction (in the aid of the Cartoon Art Trust). Please give generously!
