CAPTION2000

Saturday-Sunday 12-13 August 2000, at the Oxford Union Society, Oxford, England


CAPTION 2000 -- worth getting up for...

The rest of the world may be gripped with millennium fever, but this year Caption, the UK's best small press, alternative and underground comics event, has decided that it has seen enough of looking forward, celebrating the future, and gazing in wonderment at humankind's achievements. Instead we have decided that there's more entertainment value in grubbing around in the murky corners of our favourite genre, looking for something vile. Something scary. Something horrible.

This is your invitation to come and join us as we pass several thousand volts through the hideous, lumbering, inanimate monster that is Caption. It may be a bit pallid, smell a bit iffy, and occasionally scare the villagers, but the new reanimated Caption will be offering its usual fine mix of fearsome panels, sinister workshops, disturbing shopping, fightening comedy, and more. If that sounds like more work than your decomposing flesh can stand, there's always the option of suppurating in our sunny garden (vampires can cower safely in the bar) while partaking of our terrifyingly cheap beer...

But all this is just extra. The reason you're reading this now is that Caption needs YOU -- for its exhibition, tiny objects of fear
