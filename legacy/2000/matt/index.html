CAPTION2000

Saturday-Sunday 12-13 August 2000, at the Oxford Union Society, Oxford, England


CAPTION2000 took place on Saturday and Sunday 12-13 August 2000, in our usual venue, the Oxford Union Society. Matt Brooker took his collection of cameras to CAPTION. We have organized our selection from his pictures by the people in them (not counting some people who're obscured or not facing the camera). There also is a list of the web pictures in alphabetical order.
