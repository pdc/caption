CAPTION2000

Saturday-Sunday 12-13 August 2000, at the Oxford Union Society, Oxford, England


Meet the new committee!

At spacecaption1999 the committee announced that that was the last Caption convention they would be running. Now we can announce that there is a new CAPTION committee who will be running CAPTION2000 over the weekend of 12-13 August 2000.

The new committee is Jo Charman, Ruth O'Reilly, Ian Snell, and Alex Williams. The old committee members, Adrian Cox, Damian Cugley, Jeremy Dennis, and Jenni Scott, will still be around to offer sage advice and generally interfere from time to time.
