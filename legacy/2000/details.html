All the gruesome details

Caption 2000 is taking place in the Oxford Union, St Michael's Street (off Cornmarket Street in central Oxford). Caption runs from 10 a.m. Saturday 12 August to 5 p.m. Sunday 13 August, programme from midday Saturday.

Membership costs £5 unwaged, £8 waged, £15 Friend of Caption, £3 for Sunday only. Cheques payable to "Caption" please.

Caption is not a convention suitable for unaccompanied children.
