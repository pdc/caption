Caption Timewarp

Welcome

Caption is a festival of comics and minicomics held annually in Oxford. Join us for a weekend of panels, workshops, and idle chatter in the bar!

In the year that celebrates the 70th anniversary of the Beano’s first issue in 1938, the 30th anniversary of Bryan Talbot's seminal Adventures of Luther Arkwright, the 25th anniversary of Escape and the 20th anniversary of Deadline, Caption Timewarp will take place once again at the East Oxford Community Centre over the weekend of 9th and 10th August 2008.

Steve Whitaker (1955–2008)
