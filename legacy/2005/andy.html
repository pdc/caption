Andy was one of the best friends Caption ever had. He drew comics, designed several of our tee-shirts, did posters, promoted Caption in Dillon's window, ran workshops, gofered, drew more comics, did committee chores, and hung out in the garden in the sun talking about anything and everything to anyone and everyone.

Andy died on 18 June 2005 after being hit by a motor bike. We're creating this page to link to pictures of him at Caption and some of the work he's done at Caption, and this year's mail-art exhibition is dedicated to him.
