What is CAPTION?

Caption is a unique, friendly event that encourages small press creators, manga artists, comic-book readers and professionals to mingle and discuss their work.

  • When is CAPTION? 18–19 August 2012. That’s the weekend after the Olympics and before the Paralympics.
  • Where is CAPTION? East Oxford Community Centre, Princes Street, Oxford OX4 4BB.
  • The CAPTION table A communal pop-up shop to sell your work, freeing you to go to talks and workshops.
